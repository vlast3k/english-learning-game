# Level 09 Design: Seasons Garden

## Goal

Agent Alex reaches a secret garden where the seasons are mixed together. Gardener Mira has hidden poem clues around the garden. Alex must read the poem clues, notice what nature is doing now, and restore the garden gate.

## Curriculum Focus

- Main grammar: present continuous statements with `is/are + verb-ing`.
- Review grammar: `There is/There are`, weather and place words.
- Vocabulary: spring, summer, fall, winter, garden, poem, leaf, snow, flower, windy, rain, melt.
- Reading pocket: three short poem clues about season changes in the garden.

## Characters

- Agent Alex: the child hero.
- Gardener Mira: a friendly garden keeper who checks the season clues.

## Items And Puzzle Beats

- `spring_leaf`: A leaf clue near the garden bed. It practices `The leaf is growing` and spring vocabulary.
- `summer_flower`: A flower clue beside the warm path. It practices `The flower is opening` and summer vocabulary.
- `winter_snow_card`: A snow card near the icy gate. It practices `The snow is melting` and winter vocabulary.

## Puzzle Flow

1. Read the garden poem board.
2. Collect the spring leaf clue and answer a vocabulary/present-continuous challenge.
3. Collect the summer flower clue and answer a nature-change challenge.
4. Collect the winter snow card and answer a weather-change challenge.
5. Talk to Gardener Mira for the poem check.
6. Gardener Mira opens the garden gate.
7. Pass the exit gate review quiz.
8. Exit to the next campaign step.

## Believability Notes

- The garden gate opens only when the poem clues are ordered by season changes.
- Each clue names a visible nature change, so present continuous is meaningful: leaves are growing, flowers are opening, snow is melting.
- The guide check asks Alex to explain what is happening now in the garden.
