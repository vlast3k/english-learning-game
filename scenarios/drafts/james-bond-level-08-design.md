# Level 08 Design: Pattern Temple

## Goal

Agent Alex enters a jungle Pattern Temple. The temple gate opens only after Alex reads the pattern wall, sorts shape tiles, counts number stones, completes a sequence, and answers Keeper Maya's demonstrative check.

## Curriculum Focus

- Main grammar: demonstratives `this`, `that`, `these`, `those`.
- Review grammar: `There is/There are`, singular/plural nouns, and simple commands with `count` and `sort`.
- Vocabulary: circle, square, triangle, rectangle, star, line, dot, numbers, even numbers, odd numbers, pattern, sequence, sort, count, shapes.
- Reading pocket: a short temple wall note about shapes, numbers, and the order of a pattern.

## Characters

- Agent Alex: the child hero.
- Keeper Maya: a friendly temple keeper who checks the pattern clues.

## Items And Puzzle Beats

- `shape_tiles`: A tile set with a circle, square, triangle, and rectangle. It practices `these shapes` and sorting.
- `number_stones`: Temple stones numbered from one to six. It practices `even numbers`, `odd numbers`, and counting.
- `sequence_cards`: A pattern row with dot, line, star, then dot again. It practices `this/that` and sequence order.

## Puzzle Flow

1. Read the pattern wall mission plan.
2. Collect the shape tiles and answer a sorting/vocabulary challenge.
3. Collect the number stones and answer an odd/even counting challenge.
4. Collect the sequence cards and answer a pattern order challenge.
5. Talk to Keeper Maya for the demonstrative check.
6. Keeper Maya opens the temple gate.
7. Pass the exit gate review quiz.
8. Exit to Level 09.

## Believability Notes

- The shape tiles complete the missing temple mosaic.
- The number stones balance the gate lock by odd and even numbers.
- The sequence cards show the safe order for pressing the temple pattern.
- Keeper Maya opens the gate only after Alex can point to near and far objects with the right demonstratives.
