# Sun Temple Adventure Slice 05 Art Director Review

Reviewer role: LucasArts Senior Art Director and Game Designer.

## Verdict

Conditional Go. The ending can be emotionally strong because it does not add a new villain or another long puzzle: it turns the player's accumulated repairs into one visible return of light.

## Required Decisions

1. Keep the approach receiver to one turn-wheel and one before/after light path. Four lit route marks are background evidence, not four buttons.
2. Put the Sun Lens directly in the opened high niche. Do not delay it behind Observatory Door.
3. The complete Compass opens the Door as a persistent control object; never remove it from the player/world.
4. The great mirror turns once. The child should already understand why before clicking it.
5. The restoration must travel in the world-state order: Observatory -> Mirror Hall -> Temple -> Courtyard -> Waterfall -> Garden -> Base Camp.
6. Make the garden and Base Camp the emotional close. Do not fill every old screen with a yellow filter.

## Rejected Directions

- no final code, quiz, chase, boss, fragile beam timing, or extra collectible;
- no multiple observatory rooms with competing controls;
- no full-background remakes where an approved overlay will tell the state change;
- no ending that locks the child out of revisiting the repaired valley.

## Review State

The approach/door/observatory three-screen shape is approved for an asset plan. Prompts must be built from exact overlay footprints and the final-state plan, not generic “sunny temple” imagery.
