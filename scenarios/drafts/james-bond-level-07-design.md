# Level 07 Design: Festival Puzzle

## Goal

Agent Alex reaches a bright New Year festival square. The festival gate is closed until Alex helps prepare the celebration outfit and music clues, then answers the host's possessive-adjective check.

## Curriculum Focus

- Main grammar: possessive adjectives `my`, `your`, `his`, `her`, `our`, `their`.
- Review grammar: `have/has`, `this/these`, and `wear`.
- Vocabulary: hat, shirt, jeans, boots, clothes, colorful, music, instrument, musician, fireworks, wear, give.
- Reading pocket: a short festival board note about who wears which clothes and which instrument belongs to the musician.

## Characters

- Agent Alex: the child hero.
- Host Lina: a friendly festival host who checks the outfit and music clues.

## Items And Puzzle Beats

- `festival_hat`: A colorful festival hat near the clothing stall. It practices `my hat` and `wear`.
- `clothes_bundle`: A shirt, jeans, and boots bundle. It practices `her shirt`, `his boots`, and `clothes`.
- `music_drum`: A drum and music clue near the stage. It practices `their instrument` and `musician`.

## Puzzle Flow

1. Read the festival board mission plan.
2. Collect the festival hat and answer a vocabulary/possessive challenge.
3. Collect the clothes bundle and answer a clothes/possessive challenge.
4. Collect the music drum and answer a music/possessive challenge.
5. Talk to Host Lina for the festival check.
6. Host Lina opens the festival arch.
7. Pass the exit gate review quiz.
8. Exit to the next campaign step.

## Believability Notes

- The hat and clothes are needed because the festival has a parade outfit check.
- The drum is needed because the festival music starts when the right instrument is found.
- Host Lina opens the arch only after Alex can explain whose items are ready.
