# James Bond Level 11: City-Country Finale

## Goal

Agent Alex reads simple city and country postcards, checks everyday routines, and opens the final academy gate.

## Learning Focus

- Vocabulary: city, country, apartment, house, postcard, note, view, elevator, picnic, quiet.
- Grammar: adverbs of frequency with simple present: `always`, `sometimes`, `never`.
- Review: compare places and understand short notes.

## Story Flow

1. Read the message board to understand the finale mission.
2. Collect the city postcard clue.
3. Collect the country postcard clue.
4. Collect the routine card.
5. Talk to Agent Nora for the routine check.
6. Exit through the final academy gate.

## Kid-Readability Notes

The level text uses short present-simple sentences and concrete place words. The only new grammar load is `always/sometimes/never`, practiced in short sentences such as `I sometimes visit the city` and `We always read the notes`.

## Gate Review Words

city, country, apartment, house, postcard, note, always, sometimes, quiet, view
