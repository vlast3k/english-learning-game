# Sun Temple Adventure Slice 05 Curriculum Ledger

Status: design draft. The ending remains adventure-first and has no grammar answer gate.

## Language Policy

Use short visible-cause lines. Avoid astronomy exposition, passive voice, conditionals, and technical words such as `focus`, `receiver`, `alignment`, `observatory mechanism`, or `solar` in required text.

| Situation | English | Bulgarian |
| --- | --- | --- |
| approach clue | `The light needs to face the sky.` | `Светлината трябва да гледа към небето.` |
| receiver solved | `The light opens the niche.` | `Светлината отваря нишата.` |
| Sun Lens | `This is the Sun Lens.` | `Това е Слънчевата леща.` |
| Compass complete | `The Compass is complete.` | `Компасът е завършен.` |
| Door | `The door is open.` | `Вратата е отворена.` |
| finale | `The light found the valley.` | `Светлината намери долината.` |

## Coverage

- `be`: visible states; `The door is open.`
- simple present third person: `The light opens the niche.`
- `this`: direct object language; `This is the Sun Lens.`
- `needs to`: permitted adventure-critical clue, used once with Bulgarian support.
- core words: light, sky, door, sun, lens, mirror, open, valley.

`niche`, `valley`, and `Compass` are adventure-critical nouns with Bulgarian support; they are not grammar targets. The final slice introduces no language structure beyond the campaign scope.
