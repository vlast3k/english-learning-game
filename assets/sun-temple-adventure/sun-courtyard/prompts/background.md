# Sun Courtyard Background Prompt

Purpose: third-slice reward screen. The opened Temple Steps gate leads here; water reaches the central stone sun flower, whose open centre holds a separate Stone Lens overlay.

## Image Prompt

Use case: illustration-story.
Asset type: 16:9 painted Phaser point-and-click adventure background.

Create a warm, child-safe hand-painted open temple courtyard in the existing Sun Temple Adventure style. The single dominant focus is a large stone sun flower at the centre, already open like a friendly carved blossom. A narrow visible water channel arrives from the left or foreground gate direction and feeds the flower, making the cause-and-effect clear. Leave the flower's circular centre empty and clean for a separate Stone Lens overlay.

Composition and playability:

- 16:9 wide scene, clear lower third for character walking;
- open gate/path back to Temple Steps visible to one side;
- flower is unmistakably the centre of attention and has a clear circular lens placement area;
- warm sunlight, pale stone, green jungle edge, shallow gentle water only;
- upper corners calm for HUD and dialogue.

Required environment:

- safe open courtyard, not a dark interior;
- already-open stone flower with simple sun-shaped petals;
- visible flowing channel feeding the flower;
- one clear exit path back to Temple Steps.

Avoid:

- a painted lens, coin, gem, key, cup, mirror, tools, loose treasure, or extra flowers;
- characters, NPCs, readable text, labels, UI, or signs;
- traps, skulls, weapons, scary ruins, darkness, or franchise references.

## Overlay Notes

The Stone Lens is a separate prop overlay. Do not bake any circular object into the flower centre.
