# Shared Prop And State Atlas Slice 02 Prompt

Purpose: create clean reusable props and state overlays for the Waterfall Cave slice.

## Image Prompt

Create a painted prop/state atlas for a kid-friendly jungle adventure point-and-click game. Use a perfectly flat solid #ff00ff chroma-key background for later background removal.

The background must be one uniform #ff00ff color with no shadows, gradients, texture, reflections, floor plane, or lighting variation. Keep every prop fully separated from the background with crisp edges and generous padding. Do not use #ff00ff anywhere in the props. No cast shadows, contact shadows, reflections, watermark, or readable text.

Props should match a warm hand-painted adventure-game style and be readable at small inventory/icon sizes.

Include only these props and state elements:

1. Empty glass jar, simple and child-readable.
2. Glow leaf, a single botanical leaf with soft green-blue light, clearly a leaf and not a gem or lens.
3. Glow jar, the empty jar now holding the glow leaf and soft light.
4. Cave-lit glow overlay, a soft blue-green cave-mouth light shape with transparent-looking edges but still on the chroma background.
5. Small stone key.
6. Blue stone box shut, with a simple sun mark.
7. Blue stone box open.
8. Blue Lens, bright blue and gem-like but child-friendly, with a bright cyan rim, simple sun motif, and clean circular silhouette.
9. Blue Lens placed-in-compass overlay or circular blue slot fill.
10. Temple Steps map marker, locked/revealed style.

Requirements:

- flat #ff00ff chroma-key background;
- each prop separated with clear padding;
- no labels or readable text;
- no extra props;
- consistent lighting and style;
- simple silhouettes suitable for inventory and overlays;
- no weapons or scary symbols.

## Layout Recommendation

Arrange in a neat grid with enough spacing to crop each element. Keep inventory objects front-facing or three-quarter view.

## Exclusions

Do not include random tools, coins, books, skulls, weapons, extra jars, extra keys, extra boxes, or decorative filler.
