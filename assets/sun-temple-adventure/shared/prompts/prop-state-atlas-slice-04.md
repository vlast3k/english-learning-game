# Shared Prop And State Atlas Slice 04 Prompt

Purpose: create the transparent-ready state atlas for Mirror Hall, Base Camp Compass progress, and the future observatory forward-hook marker.

## Image Prompt

Use case: illustration-story.
Asset type: painted point-and-click game prop atlas for chroma-key removal.

Create a clean painted prop and state atlas matching the existing Sun Temple Adventure storybook style. Use a perfectly flat solid `#ff00ff` chroma-key background for later removal. The background must be one uniform color with no texture, shadow, gradient, floor, reflection, watermark, or text. Keep each element fully separated with generous padding and crisp edges. Do not use `#ff00ff` in any prop.

Arrange only these sixteen elements in a tidy, well-spaced grid:

1. Near movable mirror in its starting orientation, a warm stone-framed reflective panel on a simple sun-marked pivot mount.
2. Near movable mirror in its aligned orientation, the same panel turned to send light onward.
3. Far movable mirror in its starting orientation, visually distinct frame shape from the near mirror, with a simple pivot mount.
4. Far movable mirror in its aligned orientation, the same far panel turned toward the sun panel.
5. Narrow warm beam segment from a high source to the fixed mirror.
6. Narrow warm reflected beam segment from the fixed mirror to the near mirror.
7. Narrow warm initial beam endpoint from the near mirror to a nearby blank wall.
8. Narrow warm beam segment from the aligned near mirror to the far mirror.
9. Narrow warm intermediate beam endpoint from the far mirror to a nearby blank wall.
10. Narrow warm final beam segment from the aligned far mirror to the sun panel.
11. Opaque sealed circular sun-panel cover, honey stone with one simple sun motif and a clean central recess.
12. Open circular sun-panel state with the same outer footprint, opened stone petals or cover framing a clear central recess.
13. Small warm panel glow with a thin upward glint; not an item and not a UI marker.
14. Mirror Lens: a clear silvery-blue circular lens with a bright reflective rim and simple sun detail, clearly a lens rather than a coin, gem, button, eye, or mirror shard.
15. Mirror Lens placed-in-Compass overlay, matching the Base Camp Compass perspective.
16. Observatory Approach locked map marker: a small architectural high-path emblem with a subtle beam motif, clearly a disabled HUD destination and not a world collectible.

Requirements:

- painterly warm stone and silver-blue reflective surfaces; beams are narrow warm storybook light, never harsh lasers;
- consistent scale and three-quarter/front-facing silhouettes appropriate to the matching scene overlays and small inventory icons;
- no labels, letters, readable text, extra props, decorative filler, characters, UI panels, or cast/contact shadows;
- no weapons, traps, skulls, keys, ropes, jars, cups, coins, books, loose map pieces, extra lenses, or extra mirrors;
- all beam segments must have clean ends and gentle soft edges so they can be joined over the Mirror Hall background.
