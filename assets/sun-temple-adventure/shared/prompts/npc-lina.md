# Garden Keeper Lina NPC Prompt

Purpose: create Lina as the friendly village-garden NPC for the first vertical slice.

## Image Prompt

Create a kid-friendly painted NPC character on a transparent background: Garden Keeper Lina, a friendly village gardener who cares for seeds and flowers.

Character direction:

- warm, helpful village gardener;
- simple colorful clothes suitable for a sunny garden;
- friendly expression;
- no weapons;
- no franchise references;
- readable silhouette at Phaser sprite scale;
- should feel like a kind person who asks for help with a lost basket of seeds.

Pose needs:

- standing idle pose facing slightly toward the player;
- one hand may gesture toward the garden or flower pot;
- feet visible for placement on ground;
- transparent background.

Optional:

- include a small expression variant for happy/thankful after receiving the basket.

Avoid:

- overly detailed jewelry or props;
- holding a basket, because the basket is a separate inventory item;
- multiple garden tools that look like usable items.

## Integration Notes

Lina stands near the garden table and distinctive flower pot, but must not cover the flower pot, Green Lens reveal area, or exit back to bridge.
