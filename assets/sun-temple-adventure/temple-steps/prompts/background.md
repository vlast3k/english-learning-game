# Temple Steps Background Prompt

Purpose: third-slice temple arrival screen. Alex takes a stone cup here, then later pours water into the dry sun channel to open the gate toward Sun Courtyard.

## Image Prompt

Use case: illustration-story.
Asset type: 16:9 painted Phaser point-and-click adventure background.

Create a warm, kid-friendly hand-painted jungle temple exterior in the same bright storybook adventure style as the existing Sun Temple Adventure backgrounds. Show broad sunlit stone steps leading toward a fixed, front-facing honey-stone arch with a warm courtyard visible beyond. Stage the scene left to right: foreground-left has one low, flat, empty circular sun-marked cup rest for a future stone cup overlay; a dry carved water channel begins there, visibly runs across the ground, passes through the middle arch, and clearly continues toward the courtyard. The arch must support separate opaque closed-gate and open-gate overlays, so do not paint either gate state into the background.

Composition and playability:

- 16:9, wide, with lower third clear for character walking;
- one strong visual path from cup rest to channel to fixed arch to warm courtyard beyond;
- dry channel has a simple carved sun motif but no readable writing;
- upper corners stay calm for HUD; upper middle has room for dialogue;
- no characters, NPCs, UI, labels, signs, or text.

Required environment:

- warm honey stone, soft jungle plants at the edges, calm daylight;
- broad safe stone steps and a fixed, symmetrical front-facing arch;
- continuous dry channel in three simple mostly straight unobstructed segments: cup rest to arch, through arch, beyond arch;
- one obvious low flat empty circular cup rest, but do not paint a cup into the background;
- clean arch footprint free of vines/plants and clean channel segments free of stones, grass, deep shadows, or steps where overlays will sit.

Avoid:

- extra cups, keys, gems, ropes, tools, coins, statues, doors, or lenses;
- skulls, weapons, traps, darkness, caves, danger, horror, ruins that look threatening;
- readable inscriptions, symbols that look like text, water already flowing, a painted closed gate, or a painted open gate;
- baked UI, borders, watermark, or franchise references.

## Overlay Notes

Separate atlas overlays will later add the stone cup, three water-flow segments, and fully opaque closed/open gate states. Keep the channel, cup rest, and fixed arch surfaces clean enough for those overlays to sit over them.
