# Interactive Props Prompt

Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: Clickable prop sheet for Phaser Level 3 hotspots and inventory icons.
Primary request: Create a prop sheet of friendly village camp picnic items for an English-learning spy adventure game.
Subjects, arranged as separate non-overlapping icons in a tidy grid:
- Bread note: a parchment note with a small loaf of bread, no readable text.
- Football clue: a classic black-and-white football/soccer ball with a small clue ribbon.
- Teacher map: a folded illustrated village map on parchment, no readable text.
- Welcome board: a small wooden welcome notice board with pinned blank papers, no readable text.
- Garden table: a small round picnic/garden table with fruit bowl and cups.
- Fruit basket with apples and grapes.
- Water jug and cup.
- Picnic list parchment with simple check marks, no readable words.
Style: Polished hand-painted storybook adventure UI art matching the warm village background, crisp readable silhouettes at small inventory size, tactile wood/parchment/cloth details, safe playful spy flavor for children.
Composition: Isolated props on a light removable parchment background, each object fully visible with generous padding for cropping into Phaser hotspot art. Arrange in two rows or a clean grid. No overlaps.
Lighting/mood: Warm sunny village camp lighting, soft brush texture, clear edges.
Constraints: No character sprites, no readable text, no logos, no watermark. No guns, knives, alcohol, gambling, scary imagery, or real agency marks.
Output: Single prop sheet image, no border, no watermark.
